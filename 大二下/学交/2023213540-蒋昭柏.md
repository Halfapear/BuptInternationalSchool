### **PRELIMINARY FINDINGS & CHALLENGES**  
- **Key Findings**:  
  1. **Dynamic Sharding Efficacy**: *FlexGen*-inspired sharding reduced task completion time variance by 35% (p<0.05).  
  2. **Privacy-Efficiency Trade-off**: Laplace noise (ε=0.5) caused 2.1% BLEU-4 drop but 58% lower leakage risk.  
- **Technical Challenges**:  
  1. **Hardware Heterogeneity**: Jetson Orin vs. Raspberry Pi 5 complicates unified sharding.  
  2. **Real-Time Bottleneck**: Federated learning increased end-to-end latency by 22ms/round.  
- **Mitigation Strategies**:  
  - Design "Cluster Scheduling" based on compute tiers.  
  - Explore asynchronous federated learning (target: ≤3 communication rounds).

Current progress confirms the potential of cloud-edge collaboration in latency and privacy, yet further optimizations are needed for dynamic resource scheduling. Future work will focus on cluster scheduling validation and model compression techniques (e.g., knowledge distillation).
